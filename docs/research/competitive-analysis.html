Key Themes for the FOFC Redesign

Patterns observed across all 10 sites that should directly inform design decisions.

Warm Palette Over Clinical White

The best framing sites (Framebridge, Simply Framed) use warm off-whites and creams — not pure #FFFFFF. It mirrors the warmth of a physical frame shop.

Photography Does the Selling

Every high-performing site leads with full-bleed, high-quality imagery of real work in real spaces. FOFC has 104 photos — this is a major asset to deploy.

Longevity = Trust Signal

"20 Years" should appear above the fold, not buried in an About page. It is the single most powerful differentiator from newer or chain competitors.

One CTA Per Section

Award-winning sites (Crystal Bridges, NGA) never compete with themselves. fullPage.js sections should each have one clear action: See Gallery / Get a Quote / Visit Us.

Minimal Navigation

Top-performing sites cap navigation at 4–5 items and use hamburger menus on scroll. fullPage.js's built-in dot navigation handles this elegantly.

Satisfaction Guarantee Front & Center

Bellingham FrameWorks and Framebridge both surface their guarantee in the hero. A simple guarantee statement on FOFC would immediately build trust for first-time visitors.
